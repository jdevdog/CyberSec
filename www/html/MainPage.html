New Computer Science Major in Cyber Security




Our new major in Cyber Security provides students with a solid foundation Foundation in security fundamentals. It includes in-depth and hands-on exposure to several key areas of cybersecurity such as system/OS security, penetration testing, and software security, thus allowing the student to obtain practical experience in these areas. The major also includes coursework on cryptography from the Mathematics department, and cybersecurity law from the Business college.

Some courses required for the Cybersecurity major will only be offered in the fall or spring semesters. The following CPSC coursers will only be offered once per year.
